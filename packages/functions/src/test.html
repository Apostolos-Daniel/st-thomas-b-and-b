St Thomas B&B

Step 1: Upon your arrival at our B&B, please manually open the gate by sliding it to original-content the right. After entering, ensure the gate is closed, then proceed straight ahead and slightly diagonal to locate a set of 10 steps.

Step 2: Ascend the stairs where, on your left hand side, you will find a door painted in a serene blue.

Step 3: Adjacent to the door, discover a flower pot concealing the keys within a plastic bag. Use them to unlock the door, remembering to return the keys to their original hiding spot inside the bag within the flower pot.

Step 4: Proceed upstairs where your room awaits. Depending on your reservation, your room will be on the left with the door marked "OR", on the right with the door marked "LR", or further on the left with the door marked "WR".
